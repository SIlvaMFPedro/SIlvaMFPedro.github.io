How to use it

Manual

 On this section, we explain how the player interacts with the system and is able to play the game using VR

 When the player enters the game:

 He needs 3 objects

  • HTC VIVE VR Glasses
  • 2 Hand Controllers

 With the glasses, player will be able to see the chess board and every movements he does, while using both controllers that are shown as a Broom and a Wand










 With the wand controller, that will be on the right hand of the player, player will be able to do his moves.

 To move a piece, player will to select the piece that he wants to play, by pointing to it with the Wand. He selects it by pressing the center button of the controller and moves it by pressing again the same button, to the place he want's (inside the rules of chess).







 If player thinks he made a wrong move, he can also cancel his movement by pressing the back button of the controller and deselect the piece he selected previously by using the side button.





 Until now, was explained how player could move pieces, cancel his movements, select or deselect pieces. But he is also available to move "inside" de board, so he can see the game from another position that not the center one.

 With the broom controller, that is on player's left hand, he can check the board state by looking to it. On the board attatched to the broom, player can see all of his and opponents pieces and positions. The green dot in the middle represents the position that the player is in that moment on the board, from where he is looking to the game.




 By using the same point and click logic (of moving pieces), player can also move himself around the board, so he can see the game from another position/perspective.
